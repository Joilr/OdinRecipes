Pizza

Description

Pizza is an iconic Italian dish consisting of a thin or thick crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It's a popular and customizable dish enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted pizza toppings (e.g., pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, olives)
  • Dried oregano and red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or an inverted baking sheet dusted with cornmeal or flour to prevent sticking.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your favorite toppings.
  6. Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (or onto the preheated pizza stone if using) and bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with optional oregano and red pepper flakes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
